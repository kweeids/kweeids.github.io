Privacy Policy

On this online ordering application, we are dedicated to ensuring the privacy, confidentiality, and security of customers Personal Information. Our privacy policy sets down the rules for how we will collect, use, and disclose personal information as part of this assurance. On principal law the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), it serves as the foundation for our privacy policy. Overall, enacted to maintain, modernize, and extend existing rules and to impose new rules on private for the protection of the user’s personal information.

Introduction

As the owner, staff, and members, we assume full responsibility for the security and protection of the Personal Information within our control. To ensure compliance with our privacy policy, we have designated a specific individual who holds the responsibility for overseeing and maintaining adherence to our privacy practices. This individual serves as a point of contact for any privacy-related concerns or inquiries.

Identifying Purposes

We collect, use, and disclose Personal Information for the purpose of providing you with the specific product or service you have requested. We may use your Personal Information to offer you additional products and services that we believe may be of interest to you. The purposes for which we collect Personal Information will be clearly identified before or at the time of collection. Overall, the purpose may be evident, and your consent may be implied. Such as, when you provide your name, address, and payment information during the order process, it is understood that this information is necessary to fulfill your order.

Consent

In order to collect, use, or disclose Personal Information, we adhere to the principles of obtaining knowledge and consent, unless it is required or permitted by law. We respect the customer’s choice to provide us with their Personal Information, recognizing that it is always within their control. However, please be aware that if they choose not to provide certain information, it may limit our ability to provide you with our products or services. We do not make your consent a mandatory condition, unless it is necessary to fulfill your specific product or service request.

Limiting Collection

We will only collect Personal Information that is necessary for the identified purposes. The collection will be limited to the essential details required to fulfill those purposes. With your consent, we may collect Personal Information through various person, including in-person interactions, and correspondence via e-mail, contact number, or Facebook page.

Limiting Use, Disclosure and Retention

We strictly adhere to the principle that Personal Information shall be used or disclosed solely for the purpose for which it was originally collected, unless alternative consent has been obtained or as permitted or required by law. We will retain Personal Information only for the necessary duration to fulfill the original purpose of collection or as mandated by legal obligations. If applicable, we will disclose Personal Information to authorized third parties in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
